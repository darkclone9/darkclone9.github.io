Tool Overview

Adobe Illustrator is the industry-standard vector graphics software used for creating logos, icons, drawings, typography, and illustrations. Unlike raster-based programs, Illustrator creates scalable vector artwork that maintains crisp quality at any size.

Primary Uses:

  • Logo and brand identity design
  • Vector illustrations and artwork
  • Typography and lettering
  • Icon and symbol creation
  • Print and digital graphics
